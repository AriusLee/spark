People love to receive gifts

While a recent study showed that eight in 10 Americans are tired of irrelevant offers and coupons, Americans still love to receive gifts. Gifts feel special and motivate people to go out and use them. Receiving a Spark Gift gives consumers the freedom to spend as they wish—empowering them with choice and control.

Gifts are trusted and appreciated

To deliver gifts to people, Spark partners with leading national banks who serve millions of customers that together spend $500 billion.

A gift is received and all the customer needs to do is show up at the register to use their card. The amount is automatically credited to their debit or credit card.

Gifts are individual and relevant to consumer interest

Spark’s unique Gift Underwriting Engine determines the right gift for each individual customer to make sure each transaction is profitable for grocery and drug stores.

This engine operates under the same principles as the underwriting system used by banks to analyze loans. Spark identifies highly profitable shoppers, balances risk and ensures that the right customer is sent to grocery and drug stores.

Gifts boost shopping visits

Customers must use their Spark Gift within seven days. This sense of urgency drives immediate results for grocery and drug stores. In fact, 50 percent of Spark Gifts are used in the first 72 hours.

Spark Gift Campaigns have been used to increase visits from profitable and frequent grocery shoppers with great results.
